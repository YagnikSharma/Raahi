Uploaded surveillance videos are processed frame-by-frame through standard deep learning algorithms:

Hazard and Fire detection: Evaluates chromaticity boundaries in HSL and HSV ranges to target active fire and smoke clouds.
Luminance metrics: Computes global image histogram stats to detect localized lighting failures and blackout hazards.
