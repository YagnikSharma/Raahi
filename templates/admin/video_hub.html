How CCTV Analysis Works

Our backend offline pipeline utilizes computer vision filters to identify incidents:

  • Violence Scanning: Evaluates movement velocities and clusters to detect conflicts or physical assaults.
  • Emergency Detections: Targets pixels of fire, smoke, and blasts using HSL chroma and contours.
  • Lighting Telemetry: Assesses frame luminosity thresholds to index poor-visibility blocks.
Incidents identified in the CCTV video will be registered in your live safety database around active coordinates to simulate real-time zone alerts.
