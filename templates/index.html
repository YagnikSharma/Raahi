How Raahi Works

Our platform uses advanced computer vision algorithms to analyze CCTV footage and detect potential safety threats like fires, explosions, assaults, and poor visibility conditions.

Areas are classified into safety zones based on incident history:

  • Green - Safe (0 incidents)
  • Yellow - Caution (1-2 incidents)
  • Orange - High Caution (3-5 incidents)
  • Red - Unsafe (>5 incidents)

Anyone can access the safety map without login, helping citizens make informed travel decisions. Yellow and orange zones are for information only - use your discretion when traveling.
